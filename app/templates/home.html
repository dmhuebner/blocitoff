Bloc It Off

Welcome to Bloc It Off!


The Goal Oriented To Do List App


Bloc It Off is a simple To Do App that keeps your to do lists clean and realistic by removing items that are not completed within 7 days.

Our hypothesis is that if the task is not important enough to be completed within seven days, it does not belong on your to-do list. Expired to do items are still availble under the Expired list, but they will be cleared from your Active to do list.
